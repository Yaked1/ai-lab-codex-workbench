# Video Research Pack: Frontier Models and Multimodal Systems

Checked: 2026-07-12

This pack separates verified individual videos from YouTube discovery searches.
Verified videos were checked with `yt-dlp` for public availability, title,
channel, upload date, and `playable_in_embed=True`. A discovery search is only a
way to find candidates. It is not evidence for a model name, effort level,
price, plan, architecture, release date, or benchmark score.

The watch links below point to the repository's
[privacy-enhanced media gallery](../site/model-media.html). YouTube still serves
the stream, but the viewer stays on the GitHub Pages page. No video binary or
thumbnail is stored in Git.

## Evidence labels

- **Official video:** published by the model vendor. Useful for disclosed
  product behavior and demonstrations, but vendor benchmark claims still need
  their primary written source and method.
- **Independent test:** a third party shows prompts or workflow. Useful for
  qualitative context, not product facts.
- **Community discussion:** commentary and synthesis. Useful for discovering
  questions, not settling them.
- **Discovery search:** a precise YouTube query whose results must be checked
  before citation or embedding.

## Video-evaluation checklist

- [ ] Record upload date, channel, exact title, and stable video ID.
- [ ] Show the visible model name and effort level.
- [ ] Publish or display the full prompts and source files.
- [ ] Show complete outputs at readable resolution.
- [ ] Measure elapsed time, token or credit cost, and retries.
- [ ] Include failed attempts and recovery steps rather than editing them out.
- [ ] Use equivalent tools, context, time limits, and scoring conditions.
- [ ] Attribute every benchmark to its owner and exact version.
- [ ] Link the primary vendor source for names, prices, plans, and controls.
- [ ] Distinguish Nano Banana 2 from Nano Banana Pro.
- [ ] Distinguish Intelligence Index from Coding Agent Index.
- [ ] Check for cherry-picked prompts, outputs, or omitted failures.
- [ ] State whether a coding result includes Codex, Claude Code, or Grok Build.
- [ ] State whether Fable fallback was active.
- [ ] Treat a discovery-search result as unverified until every field is checked.

## Uncertainties

- Search order, availability, titles, and embeddability can change by region and
  date.
- A public video is not automatically licensed for redistribution.
- Edited demonstrations can hide latency, retries, and failed outputs.
- YouTube compression can invalidate fine-grained image-quality judgments.
- No verified individual Gemini Omni Flash video was added in this pass; the
  pack retains precise discovery searches instead of inventing an ID.
