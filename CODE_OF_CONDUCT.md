# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Contributors must communicate respectfully, review ideas rather than people,
and make technical disagreement specific and evidence-based. Harassment,
threats, discrimination, sexualized conduct, doxxing, deliberate humiliation,
and publication of private information are not acceptable.

## Project expectations

- Keep issues and pull requests focused on the repository.
- Do not paste credentials, private documents, private links, or personal data.
- Distinguish verified facts, observations, and opinions.
- Give beginners actionable feedback without ridicule.
- Respect licenses, attribution requirements, and content rights.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the maintainer contact method listed
in [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md). Maintainers may edit or remove content, lock
threads, reject contributions, or restrict participation when necessary to
protect contributors and the project. Reports should include links, dates, and
relevant context. Retaliation against a reporter is prohibited.

This project uses a proportionate response: clarification for minor mistakes,
formal warning for repeated problems, temporary restriction for serious or
repeated conduct, and permanent exclusion for threats, harassment, doxxing, or
other severe abuse.
